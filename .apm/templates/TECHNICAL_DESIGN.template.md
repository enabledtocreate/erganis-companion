# Technical Design

> Managed document. Must comply with template TECHNICAL_DESIGN.template.md.

- Template Version: `1.0`
- Last Updated: `2026-04-02`

## Purpose

Capture detailed implementation design for a subsystem, capability, or technical slice of the project.

## Required Sections

1. Executive Summary
2. Design Scope
3. Internal Flow
4. Data and Interface Impact
5. Risks and Tradeoffs
6. Open Questions

## Guidance

- Keep the scope narrower than architecture.
- Explain how the design should be built, not just why it exists.
